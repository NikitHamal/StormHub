About Me

Writer and poet based in Portland, Oregon. I draw inspiration from nature, urban landscapes, and the complex tapestry of human emotions. My work explores themes of identity, connection, and the search for meaning in our increasingly digital world.

Writing Journey

I've been writing since I was twelve, starting with simple journal entries that evolved into poetry and short stories. I studied Creative Writing at Portland State University and have been published in several literary magazines including The Willamette Review and Northwest Passages.

Influences

Mary Oliver, Ocean Vuong, Sylvia Plath, and Raymond Carver have all shaped my writing style and perspective.
