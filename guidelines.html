Community Guidelines

Our guidelines help maintain a supportive, respectful, and creative environment for all members.

At StormHub, we believe in fostering a community where writers and creative minds can express themselves freely while maintaining a respectful and supportive environment. These guidelines are designed to help everyone have a positive experience.

diversity_3

Respect & Inclusivity

  • Treat all community members with respect and dignity
  • Embrace diverse perspectives, backgrounds, and writing styles
  • Avoid language or content that discriminates based on race, gender, sexuality, religion, or other identities
  • Create an inclusive space where everyone feels welcome to share their voice
comment

Constructive Feedback

  • Provide thoughtful, specific feedback that helps writers improve
  • Focus on the work, not the writer
  • Balance critique with encouragement
  • Be mindful of how your feedback might be received
  • If you receive feedback, consider it with an open mind
copyright

Original Content & Attribution

  • Share only your original work or properly attribute sources
  • Respect copyright and intellectual property rights
  • When inspired by others' work, acknowledge your influences
  • Do not plagiarize or present others' work as your own
shield

Content Boundaries

  • Properly tag sensitive content (violence, trauma, mature themes)
  • Avoid gratuitous graphic content that serves no narrative purpose
  • Do not post illegal content or anything that promotes harm
  • Consider the community impact of potentially triggering material
forum

Meaningful Engagement

  • Contribute to discussions in a thoughtful manner
  • Avoid spam, excessive self-promotion, or off-topic comments
  • Listen actively to others' perspectives
  • Engage with the community beyond just sharing your own work
privacy_tip

Privacy & Boundaries

  • Respect others' privacy and personal boundaries
  • Do not share private conversations without permission
  • Be mindful of sharing personal information
  • Ask before using someone else's work or ideas as inspiration

Guideline Enforcement

Our moderation team works to ensure these guidelines are followed. Violations may result in:

  • Content removal
  • Warnings
  • Temporary restrictions
  • Account suspension in severe or repeated cases

We believe in fair moderation and will always communicate clearly about any actions taken.

Reporting Concerns

If you encounter content or behavior that violates these guidelines:

  1. Use the "Report" feature available on all content
  2. Provide specific details about the violation
  3. Our moderation team will review and respond appropriately

For urgent concerns, contact us directly at moderation@stormhub.com

These guidelines may evolve as our community grows. We welcome your feedback on how to improve them.

By participating in StormHub, you agree to follow these guidelines and help us maintain a positive, creative community for all.
